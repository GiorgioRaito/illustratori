Tim Lahan

San Francisco-based artist Tim Lahan’s naughty, witty artwork is the product of his ability to digest and illustrate clever glitches, concepts and interesting visual cues from the world around him. “I generally aim to make things as simple as possible,” says Tim“Sometimes they come out funny”.
the ADC Young Gun award winner’s instantly recognisable style is enviably simple and genuinely funny: something very few illustrators can boast.
Tim Lahan is an illustrator known for having created various advertising campaigns such as that of Kenzo which represented big eyes on backgrounds with psychedelic colors, a bit like 13th Floor Elevators.
In fact, Tim's creations seem to come from a delirious LSD trip (apparently taken well) of bright colors and rainbows that convey a great joie de vivre.
The power of his light and carefree language lends itself very well to any type of representation.

Tim Lahan is a graphic artist and illustrator based in the USA who splits his time between commercial work and personal projects that include everything from self-publishing to painting.
His work has been featured/collected in many different places in print, online, and in the physical world including places like Printed Matter, The New York Public Library, and the MoMA Library.
